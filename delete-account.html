Collection Tracker — Delete Account & Data

Collection Tracker gives you full control over your data. You can delete your data directly within the app or request account deletion by contacting us.

Option 1: Delete Your Data from Within the App

You can delete individual collections, items, and images at any time directly within the app. To delete all local data at once:

  1. Open Collection Tracker
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) on the main Collections screen
  3. Select Dev Mode
  4. Tap Delete All Data
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This will permanently delete all collections, items, field values, images, and related data stored locally on your device.

Option 2: Request Full Account & Cloud Data Deletion

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated cloud data, send an email to:

collectiontrackerapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Collection Tracker account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

Data Type Deleted
Firebase Authentication account Yes — permanently removed
Collections, items, and field values (Firestore) Yes — permanently removed
Photos and images (Firebase Storage) Yes — permanently removed
Appearance and sync settings (Firestore) Yes — permanently removed
Subscription status Managed by Google Play — cancel via Google Play subscriptions
Important: Account and cloud data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Local data on your device is not affected by a cloud deletion request — use Option 1 above to clear local data. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before requesting account deletion.

Data Retention

Upon receiving a deletion request, all cloud-stored data (Firestore documents, Firebase Storage files, and your Firebase Authentication account) will be permanently deleted within 30 days. No data is retained after deletion is complete.
